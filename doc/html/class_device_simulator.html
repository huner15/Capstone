Detailed Description

A DeviceSimulator is an abstract representation of an aircraft surveillance device simulator. A device simulation occurs on a separate TCP/IP network port and is designed to exist on a separate thread. The simulation behavior is dependent on the behavior of SendReports() which in turn is dependent on the behavior of SendReport().
