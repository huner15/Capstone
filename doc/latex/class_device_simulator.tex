\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \hyperlink{class_device_simulator}{Device\+Simulator} is an abstract representation of an aircraft surveillance device simulator. A device simulation occurs on a separate T\+C\+P/\+I\+P network port and is designed to exist on a separate thread. The simulation behavior is dependent on the behavior of \hyperlink{class_device_simulator_a2c63437b2c443a7c1e7dcda80c5efe77}{Send\+Reports()} which in turn is dependent on the behavior of \hyperlink{class_device_simulator_ac41c9ef2046591fe7f62b55ef5f0de22}{Send\+Report()}.
